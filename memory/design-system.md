# Brand Identity

Name: CBS IT Union Website

## Brand Description
CBS IT Union is a student-led organization at Copenhagen Business School that connects students across Data Science, Digital Business, and Information Systems.

It represents the intersection of:
- Technology
- Business
- Student community

Core purpose:
- Enable collaboration
- Foster innovation
- Create career opportunities

---

## Brand Personality

Primary adjectives:
- Modern
- Collaborative
- Approachable
- Structured
- Dynamic

Secondary traits:
- Professional but not corporate
- Energetic but not chaotic
- Student-driven but credible

## Voice & Tone

### Voice
- Clear
- Modern
- Student-focused

### Tone
- Friendly but professional
- Inclusive and accessible
- Confident without being corporate

---

## Component Style Preferences

### Buttons
- Style: solid primary color
- Shape: rounded (soft edges)

### Cards
- Clean, minimal
- Light shadows or none
- Medium padding

### Visual Style
- Clean and structured layouts
- Balanced whitespace
- Avoid clutter

---

## Design Principles

- Prioritize clarity over creativity
- Use familiar UI patterns (Jakob’s Law)
- Limit options (Hick’s Law)
- Group content in 3–4 items
- Maintain consistency across pages

---

## Do / Don’t Rules

### Do
- Use official color palette consistently
- Keep typography consistent
- Use structured layouts
- Maintain strong contrast

### Don’t
- Use random colors outside palette
- Over-design or clutter layouts
- Modify logo colors arbitrarily
- Mix inconsistent styles

---

## Key Insight

The brand should feel like:

→ a modern student tech community  
NOT → a corporate institution  

Balance:
- professionalism  
- energy  
- accessibility
